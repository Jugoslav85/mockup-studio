How it works

Three steps to perfect mockups

No learning curve. No tutorials. Just upload, generate, download.

01 — Upload

Pick your template

Choose a free PSD template from our library — or upload your own. Poster frames, wall art, apparel, packaging and more.

02 — Drop

Add your designs

Drop in your artwork — one image or fifty. The app maps each design to your template automatically and lets you reorder with a drag.

03 — Done

Download your mockups

Hit Start Batch. Every design is rendered inside your template and packaged for download in seconds. PNG or JPEG, your choice.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Any PSD template

Use templates from our free library or upload your own PSD files. Any template with smart object layers works perfectly.

True batch processing

Upload 50 designs at once and generate all mockups in a single run. Every combination, automatically matched and rendered.

Fast — seriously fast

Powered by Photopea running silently in the background. Each mockup takes seconds, not minutes. No waiting, no queues.

PNG or JPEG output

Choose your output format and JPEG quality. Download individual mockups or your entire batch as a single ZIP file.

Fit, Fill, or Stretch

Control exactly how your design sits inside the template — per image. Perfect for portraits, landscapes, and square designs alike.

Runs in the browser

Nothing to download or install. Works on Mac, Windows, and Linux. Your files never leave your computer.
